Endure

Yes , it is lonely . Yes , it is hard . Yes , it is difficult . Yes , it is challenging . But it is worth it . If you want to be successful , you have to endure the pain of discipline , the pain of sacrifice , the pain of hard work . On the way up it just get lonelier , but to be the one in a million , to set yourself from the crowd , endure it .

Achieve

Nothing is out of your reach , nothing is impossible . If you can dream it , you can achieve it . You can be anything you want to be , have anything you desire , accomplish anything you set out to accomplish - if you will hold to that desire with singleness of purpose . Really , no better feeling than conquering what everyone told you was impossible .
